Refunds and Returns Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Thank you for purchasing from Red Team Leaders. This Refunds and Returns Policy explains how refund requests, cancellations, exchanges, and exceptions apply to our digital courses, training programs, and related educational products.

Quick summary: Refund requests for eligible course purchases may be submitted within 7 days after purchase. Because our products are primarily digital, eligibility depends on the amount of content accessed, downloaded, attended, or completed, as described below.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to digital educational products sold through Red Team Leaders, including but not limited to:

  • Self-paced online courses
  • Recorded training libraries
  • Live online classes or cohort-based programs
  • Downloadable course resources, labs, templates, and study materials
  • Bundles, memberships, and promotional course offers, unless otherwise stated at checkout

2. Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund within 7 calendar days after purchase, provided the course or product remains eligible. To qualify for a refund, all of the following generally apply:

  • The request must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • The course must not have been substantially accessed, consumed, downloaded, or completed.
  • No certificate of completion must have been issued for the course.
  • The account must not show signs of abuse, content copying, redistribution, credential sharing, or policy manipulation.
  • Proof of purchase, such as the order confirmation or invoice, must be provided upon request.

3. Digital Product Access and Exceptions

Because Red Team Leaders sells digital courses and training content, certain purchases may become non-refundable once meaningful access or use has occurred. A refund request may be denied if, for example:

  • A substantial portion of the course content has already been viewed or completed.
  • Downloadable materials, private resources, lab files, or bonus content have been accessed or downloaded.
  • A completion certificate, proof of participation, or similar credential has already been issued.
  • The product was purchased as a final sale, limited offer, special promotion, or otherwise marked non-refundable at checkout.
  • The customer has already attended or started a live cohort, workshop, mentoring session, or scheduled training event.

4. Course Transfers and Exchanges

Since digital courses are generally not returned in the traditional sense, Red Team Leaders may, at its sole discretion, offer an alternative solution instead of a refund, such as:

  • Transfer to another course of equal or similar value
  • Transfer to a future cohort or intake date
  • Store credit for future training purchases
  • Technical support to resolve an access issue instead of processing a refund

Any exchange, transfer, or credit is subject to approval and may depend on course format, availability, pricing differences, and how much of the original course has been used.

5. Technical Issues and Access Problems

If you experience a genuine technical problem preventing access to a purchased course, please contact us before requesting a chargeback or dispute. We will make reasonable efforts to restore access, correct the issue, or provide an appropriate solution.

Refunds are not automatically granted for temporary technical issues caused by internet connectivity, device compatibility, browser settings, third-party platforms, firewalls, VPN issues, or user-side configuration problems, especially where access can be restored with reasonable support.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable unless required by applicable law or expressly stated otherwise by Red Team Leaders:

  • Courses substantially viewed, completed, or consumed
  • Downloaded digital files, private materials, tools, templates, or bonus resources
  • Live training sessions already attended, in full or in part
  • Coaching, mentoring, consulting, or one-on-one educational sessions already delivered or scheduled within the active refund period
  • Gift cards, vouchers, memberships already used, and promotional or final-sale products
  • Purchases flagged for fraud, abuse, unauthorized payment activity, or account sharing

7. Refund Process

Once we receive your request, we will review the order details, account activity, course access history, and any relevant circumstances before approving or rejecting the refund.

  • Approved refunds are generally issued to the original payment method.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your card issuer, bank, or payment provider.
  • Any applicable taxes, transaction handling rules, or legally required adjustments may affect the final refunded amount.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, exchange, or course transfer, please contact our support team and include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your purchase email address
  • Your order number or payment reference
  • The name of the course or product purchased
  • The reason for your request
  • Any screenshots or details relevant to technical access problems, if applicable

9. No Physical Returns

Because our main products are digital courses and online educational services, there is usually no physical return shipment involved. Customers should not mail any physical item unless Red Team Leaders specifically requests it.

10. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you believe there has been a billing error, duplicate charge, or unauthorized transaction, please contact us first so we can investigate and attempt to resolve the matter promptly. Filing a chargeback after receiving and substantially using digital course content may result in suspension or termination of access while the matter is under review.

11. Consumer Rights

This policy does not limit any mandatory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws. Where local law grants additional cancellation, withdrawal, refund, or digital-content rights, those rights will apply to the extent required.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Refunds and Returns Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our courses, payment processes, legal requirements, or business operations. Any updated version becomes effective once posted on this page.
